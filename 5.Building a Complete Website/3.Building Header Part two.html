Achievement of the university

Early Career Achievement: Given to a recent Kinesiology alumnus who is excelling in a field related to Kinesiology. The 2017 awardees are Nick Shaw and Mike Israetel. Career Achievement: Given to a Kinesiology alumnus who has shown outstanding professional and personal achievement throughout their career in their chosen field and/or public service in any field. The 2017 awardees are José Kottoor and Melva Thompson-Robinson.
